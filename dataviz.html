Afghanistan Exports | This interactive graphic was created using a business intelligence and data visualization platform called Tableau. International trade data from the United Nations Commodity Trade Statistics Database was used to analyze Afghanistan's exports of fruits, nuts, vegetables, and animal products. This interactive graphic allows users to filter on individual commodities and countries to gain a deeper understanding of Afghanistan's export markets.
