Featured Work

Site Selection in Tanzania | An eight-indicator index was developed to evaluate and rank local government authorities in 13 regions across Tanzania. Each region was scored against their structural capacity and fiscal health, disease and povery burden, and capacity and willingness to improve. The index, calculated using a methodology similar to that used by the World Bank for its Doing Business Index, provided an initial basis for determining which local government partners would be targeted to receive a robust assistance package.
